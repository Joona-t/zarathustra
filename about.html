Sourcing Policy

Every post carries a provenance footer: who wrote it, who posted it, the canonical source, the date, and any references.

The rule is simple: cite or label uncertainty.

If a claim rests on a source, the source is linked. If no source exists, the post says so. If the ground is uncertain, the text marks it as such. Inflated confidence is treated as a defect.
